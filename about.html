About JobTrack

JobTrack was created to simplify the job search and give job seekers clarity, structure, and confidence throughout their career journey. Applying for jobs can quickly become overwhelming, applications spread across different platforms, missed follow-ups, forgotten interview dates, and lost feedback. JobTrack brings everything together in one clean, easy-to-use space.

With JobTrack, users can track where they've applied, monitor application statuses, manage interview schedules, record feedback, and stay on top of follow-ups. Instead of relying on spreadsheets, notes, or memory, JobTrack helps you stay organized and focused on what matters most, progress.

JobTrack is built for students, graduates, and professionals at any stage of their career who want to approach their job search with intention and consistency. Whether you're applying for your first role or planning your next career move, JobTrack supports you every step of the way.

Our goal is simple: to help you stay organized, reduce stress, and move closer to the opportunities you deserve.
